System Requirements

  • Supported Platforms: X86, X64, ARM.
  • Supported Operating Systems: Windows. Fully Supports M-Chip Macs via Windows 11 ARM Emulation.
  • Minimum Requirements (X86/64): Windows 10 X86, DirectX 9 Compatible Graphics, 768MB RAM, Dual-Core Processor, 60MB of Free SSD Space in NTFS Format, or 700MB in ExFAT.
  • Recommended Specs (X86/64): Windows 11, DirectX 9 Compatible Graphics with 128MB VRAM, 6GB RAM, i7 4770K or Ryzen 5 1500X Processor, 2GB of Free SSD Space in NTFS Format, Internet Connection.
  • Recommended Specs (ARM): Windows 11 ARM, Integrated Graphics, 4GB Unified RAM, Multi-Threading Support, 2GB of Free SSD Space, Internet Connection.
  • Required Software: Microsoft .NET Framework 9.0

MyCompass: A Bundle of Applications

Budget Helper

Financial Planning Made Simple.

•    Detailed Budget Tracking: Simulate and manage your finances with tools that let you track recurring expenses, income, and more.

•    Date-Range Analysis: Select specific date ranges to analyze your spending patterns and calculate daily spending limits.

•      Visualization: Use the built-in daily insights table to quickly see your financial wellbeing at a glance.

•    Export Options: Save your budget data as a CSV file for use in Excel or sync it directly with Google Sheets.

•    Paycheck Management: Break down your finances by paycheck and get a clear picture of what happens between paydays.

InScribe

Journaling, Distraction-Free Note-Taking, and Memory Tracking.

•    Rich Text Editing: Write journal entries or take notes with tools for formatting text, inserting photos, and embedding files. Whether it’s a diary entry or an important document, you can keep everything in one place.

•    Memory Tags: Mark specific entries as "memories," which get saved into a separate database for easy retrieval.

•    Algorithmic Search: Find specific memories or journal entries by searching through your text algorithmically, using a similarity bias of your choosing. 

•    Collaborative Editing: Work on shared journals or projects with real-time multi-user editing through MyCompass Cloud.

•    Organized Structure: Manage your writing, visualized as books and pages, all accessible from a simple sidebar interface.

Daily Check-In

Schedule and Routine Building

•   Task Management: Create daily schedules with categories, tasks, and subtasks. Each task can include notes, icons, and descriptions for clarity.

•   Reusable Templates: Save entire categories or task setups as templates, making it easy to repeat routines or schedules.

•    Visual Feedback: Tasks and categories automatically change color based on their completion status—red for incomplete, green for complete.

•    Night View and Customization: Adjust the interface for better readability in low light, or customize task icons and colors for personal preferences.

•    Touch Screen Support: Designed to be compatible with low-power signage applications, like AIO PCs, to create a tangible-yet-digital reminder system.

* Daily Check-In is in the Alpha prototype stage. It's stable, but the UI is under heavy development.
